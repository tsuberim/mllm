Architecture

GPT-style decoder-only transformer. Three configs from sanity-check to production.

Key choices: RMSNorm, SwiGLU MLP, weight tying, pre-norm, no bias on linear layers.

Stack

PyTorch (training, CUDA) MLX (inference, Metal) Triton (planned kernels) CoreML (iPhone, planned) tiktoken GPT-2 FineWeb-Edu Knowledge Distillation W&B HuggingFace Hub

PyTorch is the source of truth. MLX is inference-only with custom Metal kernels. Weight conversion is explicit and parity-tested at atol=1e-6.
